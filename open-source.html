The Debut Sessions

Open-Source Initiative

The Debut Sessions has created the 'Open-Source Initiative' to enable every investor in the ecosystem to have sight of the applications submitted, subject to the Founders opting in. Inclusivity is not always something prevalent across our industry, so we want to make sure every founder has the maximum chance of gaining exposure to active investors.
